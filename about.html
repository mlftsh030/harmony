About Us

Founded in 2023 by Mihle Harmony Gabuza, Harmony Travel and Tours is committed to providing exceptional travel experiences in Cape Town and the surrounding Western Cape cities. Guided by our slogan, "Travel in Tranquility," we aim to deliver seamless, enjoyable journeys that showcase the best of our vibrant region.

Our mission is to offer premium transportation and tour services that ensure a tranquil and enjoyable travel experience. Committed to exceptional customer service, we strive to be the preferred choice for travelers seeking unparalleled comfort, reliability, and unforgettable journeys.
